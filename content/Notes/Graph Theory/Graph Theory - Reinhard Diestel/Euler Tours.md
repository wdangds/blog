## 1. Key Ideas

> [!definition] Euler Tour
> A **closed walk** that goes through **every edge exactly once**, starting and ending at the same vertex.

> [!Eulerian Graph]
> A graph that has an Euler tour.

> Idea: we can draw the whole graph without lifting the pencil and without retracing any edge, ending where we started.

> [!important] Euler's Theorem
> A connected graph is **Eulerian if and only if every vertex has even degree**.

> [!warning] Necessity
> If a vertex is traversed by an Euler tour, it must use an equal number of incoming and outgoing edges, resulting in an even degree.
